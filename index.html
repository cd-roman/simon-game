Gameplay

Press any button on your keyboard to start the game. Once started, the game will highlight the first button of the sequence.

Your challenge is to memorize and replicate the entire sequence by clicking the buttons in the exact order they were shown. As you successfully complete each level, the challenge increases by adding another button to the sequence, making the game progressively more engaging and testing your memory further.

You can turn on/turn off the background music by clicking on the sound emoji located at the bottom right corner of the screen.
